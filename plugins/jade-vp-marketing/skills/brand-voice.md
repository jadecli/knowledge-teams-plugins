# Brand Voice Skill

## Activation

Auto-fires when generating marketing copy, press releases, or external
communications.

## Brand voice principles

- **Direct**: No jargon, no buzzwords, say what you mean.
- **Technical but accessible**: Respect the reader's intelligence.
- **Confident without arrogance**: Back claims with data.
- **Human**: Write like a thoughtful person, not a corporation.

## Tone by channel

| Channel | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Blog | Educational, conversational |
| Docs | Precise, structured |
| Social | Punchy, direct |
| Email | Personal, action-oriented |
| Press | Formal, factual |

## Anti-patterns to avoid

- "Leveraging synergies"
- "Best-in-class"
- "Disruptive"
- "AI-powered" (show, don't tell)
- Passive voice overuse
