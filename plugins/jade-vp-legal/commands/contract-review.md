# /contract-review

Reviews a contract document and flags clauses that require legal attention,
negotiation, or human lawyer review.

## Risk levels

| Level | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 🔴 Critical | Do not sign — engage lawyer |
| 🟡 High | Negotiate before signing |
| 🟢 Medium | Note and monitor |
| ⚪ Info | Standard clause, no action |

**DISCLAIMER**: This is AI-assisted review, not legal advice.
Always engage a qualified lawyer for binding agreements.
