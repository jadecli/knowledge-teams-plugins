# Working Backwards from Customer

## Activation

Auto-fires when designing new features, products, or strategies. Apply
whenever the team is at risk of building inside-out (technology-first) rather
than outside-in (customer-first).

## The Process

### Step 1: Write the Press Release (PR/FAQ)
Imagine the product is already launched. Write:
- **Headline**: What is this, in one sentence?
- **Summary**: Problem solved, for whom.
- **Problem**: What pain exists today?
- **Solution**: How does this solve it?
- **Quote (customer)**: Fictional but realistic.
- **Call to action**: What does the customer do next?

### Step 2: FAQ
Answer every hard question a skeptic would ask:
- Why would a customer pay for this?
- Why can't they do this already?
- Why are we the right team?
- What's the biggest risk?

### Step 3: Validate before building
Share PR/FAQ with 3 real or potential customers before writing a line of code.
Revise until the customer quote rings true.

### Step 4: Work backwards to spec
From the PR/FAQ, derive:
- User stories
- Success metrics
- Technical requirements (in that order)
