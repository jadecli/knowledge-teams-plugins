# /mbr

Conducts a **Monthly Business Review** (MBR) — deeper than WBR, includes
trend analysis, variance explanations, and 30/60/90-day forward outlook.

## Structure

1. **Scorecard** — MoM and YoY comparisons for all S-Team metrics.
2. **Variance analysis** — Explain >10% deviations from plan.
3. **Customer spotlight** — 1–2 customer stories (win/churn).
4. **Forward outlook** — 30/60/90-day forecast.
5. **Action register** — Outstanding items from last MBR.

## Output

Produces `docs/reviews/mbr-{MONTH}.md`.
